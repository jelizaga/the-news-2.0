San Francisco Prep School Suspends 14 Students For Attending Race-Themed Party

posted January 29, 2016
  • St. Ignatius College Preperatory in San Francisco, CA, has suspended 14 students.
  • They were wearing clothes meant to mimic urban black culture at a party.
  • The alleged theme of the party was "wigger."
  • The president of the high school sent their parents a letter informing them that the party had "racial overtones and racist implications."
  • It is incredibly sad that this type of thing exists not just for St. Ignatius, but for the entire city. - Patrick Ruff, principal of St. Ignatius College Preperatory.
  • The party was not organized by students of St. Ignatius.
